About Me

I am a high-achieving high school senior of Korean descent from Andover, Massachusetts. I was born in Boston, MA to Daniel and Minji Kim. I have one younger sister named Amanda who is currently a freshman at Andover High School. At 4 years old, my family and I moved to Andover, at which time I entered the Andover Public Schools system. In my free time, I like to watch Korean dramas, play tennis, and go shopping.

Tennis

I am a competitive tennis player who plays for the Willows Racquet Club girls' team year-round and the Andover High School girls' varsity team during the spring season. I practice three times a week, and I play matches on the weekends. In 2018, my doubles partner and I were the MA D1 North Doubles Finalists and we received the Boston Globe and Boston Herald All-Scholastic honors. As a student-athelete, I consistenly demonstrate exceptional time management skills, dillegience, determination, leadership, and integrity.

Service

Service has always been an important part of my family's life as well as my own. Every Friday evening, I volunteer for Awana, a program at my church for children. I help supervise game time and help the children memorize Bible verses. In the summer, I also serve at my church's Vacation Bible School. In the fall and spring, my high school tennis coach runs a tennis clinic for the youth in the community. I love volunteering as an assistant coach because I get to interact with children and help them learn my favorite sport. This February, my dad and I will be going on a short-term mission trip with a group from our church to Guatemala. We will be serving at a local children's camp for a week.

Graphic Design

I have a strong passion for designing flyers, posters, logos, cards, book page layouts, and anything that has to do with graphic design. This passion sparked when I took Graphic Design sophomore year. I really enjoyed the projects and I even got an Underclassmen Award for that class. Outside of the classroom, I have designed flyers for Aaron's Presents and I also helped design Willows' team gear. I am hoping to continue graphic design in some way in college.
